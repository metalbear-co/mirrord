`mirrord operator status` and `mirrord session list` no longer show preview-env entries. Previews are still surfaced in the local `mirrord ui` and browser extension via `MirrordOperator.status.sessions`, but the CLI's session-management surfaces hide them so the displayed ids, ports, and queue-splitting state always reflect real exec sessions. Long-term unification of previews and exec sessions is tracked separately.
