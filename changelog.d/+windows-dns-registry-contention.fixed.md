Fixed concurrent DNS resolution intermittently failing under mirrord on Windows. A burst of in-flight async `GetAddrInfoExW` queries, such as a .NET `HttpClient` issues under load, could exhaust the managed-handle registry's single-lock retry budget and drop a query, which surfaced to the app as a `WSAHOST_NOT_FOUND` `SocketException`. The registry now shards its lock across independent shards, so concurrent registration no longer serializes on one lock.
